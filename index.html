Who We Are

Built to Compete.
Driven to Inspire.

Ctrl-Alt-Defeat (Team #31844) is an FTC robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. We are a group of students passionate about engineering, coding, and community.

Our name says it all — we don't just compete, we come to rewrite the outcome. Every match, every build, every line of code is our way of hitting Ctrl-Alt-Defeat on the problem in front of us.

Foundational Legacy

From Lego League Roots to Tech Challenge Heights

Before stepping into FTC, our engineering foundations were forged in FIRST LEGO League (FLL). This early experience in block coding, modular mechanics, and rapid iteration taught us how to systematically solve complex challenges.

Today, we carry that legacy forward. We don't just look back at our FLL journey—we actively pay it forward by mentoring, coaching, and providing technical support to local FLL teams, building a strong, sustainable pipeline for the next generation of engineers.
