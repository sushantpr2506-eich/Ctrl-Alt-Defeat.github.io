Intake System

Mechanism 01
Intake System
Our intake system features a roller mechanism mounted at the front of the robot, paired with a funnel to guide balls smoothly into the robot. The compact design keeps the center of gravity low while allowing for fast and consistent ball collection during both autonomous and driver-controlled periods.
  • Roller intake designed for fast and reliable ball collection
  • Funnel guides balls smoothly into the robot
  • Wide intake opening allows successful pickups even when slightly misaligned
  • Prioritizes consistency to prevent any over capacity issues
