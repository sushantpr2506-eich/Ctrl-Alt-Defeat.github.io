Mechanisms

A Closer Look

Mechanism 01
Intake System
Our intake collects game elements into the robot efficiently and consistently. The roller intake is designed for fast and reliable ball collection with a wide opening to handle slightly misaligned pickups.
  • Roller intake designed for fast and reliable ball collection
  • Funnel guides balls smoothly into the robot
  • Wide intake opening allows successful pickups even when slightly misaligned
  • Prioritizes consistency to prevent any over capacity issues
Mechanism 02
Current Design
Our robot's electronics and control system are mounted centrally on the frame, keeping wiring organized and accessible for quick fixes between matches. Components are carefully positioned to maintain balance and allow easy access during repairs or adjustments.
  • Custom design enhanced for our game strategy
  • Faster cycle times due to improved intake
  • Built using lessons learned from our early season starterbot
  • Averaging 17 balls per match in TeleOp
