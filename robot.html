Engineering & Design

Our Robots

A look under the hood at our engineering iteration, mechanical subsystems, and code architecture across seasons.

Current Season

DECODE Robot (2025–2026)

Our active iteration focusing on speed, precision scoring, and high-reliability mechanisms.

DECODE Bot v1

Designed in Autodesk Fusion and built with custom aluminum channel framing and custom 3D printed components.

  • Drivetrain4-Motor Mecanum
  • Control SystemREV Control Hub
  • VisionWebcam + AprilTags
  • Programming LanguageJava (FTC SDK)

Drivetrain Subsystem

Mecanum wheel setup enabling precise 360-degree holonomic movement for quick repositioning during autonomous and teleop.

Active Intake

High-speed compliant roller intake engineered for swift game piece pickup and jam-free transferring.

Lift System

Multi-stage telescopic linear slides powered by high-torque motors for smooth, rapid extension.

Autonomous Code

Custom autonomous paths utilizing Odometry tracking and AprilTag alignment for pinpoint consistency.

Rookie Season

CENTERSTAGE Robot (2024–2025)

Our inaugural FTC robot that established our team's mechanical baseline and programming stack.

Rookie Bot

Built to handle pixel scoring on the backdrop and rigging hanging during our rookie competition season.

  • DrivetrainMecanum Drive
  • Scoring SubsystemLinear Slide + Pixel Box
  • EndgameRigging Hook / Suspension
  • SensorsDrive Encoders + Color Sensor

Custom Chassis

Compact frame layout designed specifically to fit within sizing constraints while maintaining a low center of gravity.

Pixel Intake & Outtake

Dual-pixel gripper paired with a multi-stage linear slide to score at high backdrop levels efficiently.

Rigging Mechanism

Endgame lift hook allowing the robot to perform a full hanging maneuver before match expiration.

Encoder TeleOp

Driver-centric controls with smooth power curves, automated slide positioning presets, and intake macros.
