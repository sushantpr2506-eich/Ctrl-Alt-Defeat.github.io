What We Did

A look at the outreach events we ran and participated in our first season.

School Visit
Walnut Hills Elementary
⏱ Robotics at School
We visited Walnut Hills Elementary to introduce students to FTC robotics — presenting our robot, explaining how it works, and inspiring the next generation of engineers.
FLL Coaching
Coaching Team #26808
⏱ FLL Mentorship
We mentored and coached FLL Team #26808, helping younger students learn to build and program robots, develop their engineering notebook, and prepare for competition.
