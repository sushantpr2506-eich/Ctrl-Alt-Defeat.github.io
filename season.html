Game Overview

The DECODE 2025–26 challenge presented complex autonomous pathing and rapid cycling mechanics. Teams were tasked with navigating field obstacles, scoring game elements into tiered structures during both the autonomous and driver-controlled periods, and securing end-game hanging or parking bonuses. Our design focused on high-speed intake efficiency and consistent auto routines.
